 Welcome to Ford's K-grams page! Ford is one of the schools K-grams is partnered with in the Willow Run District and is by far one of the most active.  Each year Ford and K-grams do tons of great projects together including mock elections and jeopardy and for the past two years, Ford kids have come to U of M for K-Day - a day of fun and excitement that includes such awesome things as a tour of the Big House, visits from athletes and enjoying lunch in a residence hall (imagine all the cookies you can possibly eat!).
