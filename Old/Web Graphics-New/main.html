K-grams Recess!!

Do you miss four-square? Do you wish that you could have recess during your Chem 101 Lecture? Here's your chance to take a break and act like a kid again!

K-grams is teaming up with Comcast and the Game Show Network to bring Extreme Dodgeball to campus. On Thursday, September 2 from 11am-5pm bring your friends, your roommates, or the random people you meet on the street to the Diag and see if you have what it takes to be Find out more about K-grams Welcome Week event.

Apply to be a K-grams Pen Pal!

Now is the time to get involved on campus. If you live in the Residence Halls you can apply to be a K-grams pen pal! As a pen pal you have a chance to act as a mentor, meet an awesome little kid, and make friends in your Residence Hall. Find out about how you can apply to be a pen pal here.

Other ways to get involved...

There are many other ways to become involved in K-grams! Starting in late September, K-grams BookMARK, offers opportunities for any University of Michigan student to go into an elementary classroom and mentor a kid. Sign-ups will begin in late September, but you can learn more about becoming a BookMARK volunteer here.

If you are interested in becoming more involved with K-grams, you can e-mail smile@umich.edu for further information. We can't wait to see you in the Fall!
