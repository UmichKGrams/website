Gearing Up for the Winter!

K-grams would like to thank all of its pen pals and volunteers for making this semester a wonderful experience for so many little kids! Together you wrote over 3,000 letters and visited the classrooms over 500 times!!! Think of how many countless smiles you were the cause of this semester! We hope that you are ready for even more K-grams fun in the upcoming semester.

Pen pal letters are due by 8pm on Monday, January 10th to the boxes in residence hall libraries or community learning centers. Remember that kids love stickers and lots of color!

K-grams BookMARK offers opportunities for any U-M student to go into an elementary classroom to mentor and read with kids in a more direct setting. BookMARK sign-ups for the winter semester will be available in early Janary. Please email bookmark@umich.edu if you have any questions.

K-grams Family and Friends Gym Night

Friday, January 21st, 2005 at 7:00pm will be the Third Annual K-grams Family and Friends Gym Night at the Michigan Women's Gymnastics Meet at Crisler Arena! As the Wolverines battle the Penn State Nittany Lions, K-grams will bring the fun!

All K-grams kids, families, and friends are cordially invited to attend this special event. University of Michigan students get in to the event FREE with their M-Card. This will be a unique opportunity to see two rival teams compete, hang out in Crisler Arena, and have fun with all of your K-grams friends.

K-grams Seventh Annual Kids-Fair

On March 11th, 2005 over 1,000 1st-5th grade students from nine schools in Ann Arbor, Detroit, and Ypsilanti will come to the University of Michigan campus for the Seventh Annual K-grams Kids-Fair.

Crisler Arena will host all of these students, over 1,000 college buddies, and over 100 student groups who will come together to celebrate the entire year of reading, writing, swingin', and smilin'! K-grams is now recruiting student groups to host booths and college buddies to hang out with the kids during the day!

Other ways to get involved...

If you are interested in becoming more involved with K-grams, email smile@umich.edu for further information. We can't wait to see you soon.

Please be sure to continue checking our Calendar of Events for K-grams happenings.
