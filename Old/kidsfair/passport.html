Pen Pal Passport Pictures!
This year, Kids-Fair is taking the kids on a journey around the world with an international theme. Kids-Fair 2002: "Smilin' Around the World" is incorporating activities and project ideas that involve geography, different cultures and customs, languages, foods, and various methods of travel.
We are putting together a special passport for each little pen pal to use at Kids-Fair and to have as a keepsake of K-grams. This passport will include a photograph of you!  We need you to TURN IN A PHOTOGRAPH (no larger than 4x6) of yourself. You can turn this picture in to the letter drop box in your residence hall library. Pictures are due to the letter drop boxes in your residence hall library by Monday, December 3rd.

Be sure to include the following on the back of the photograph:
1. Your first name and last initial
2. Your pen pal's name
3. Your pen pal's teacher
4. Your room # and rez hall

A couple of things to keep in mind when you turn in your pictures . . .

* Please do not turn in pictures taken from a webcam.
* You can turn in a picture of yourself with other people in it. Just be sure to tell us who you are in the picture.

If you have any questions about this, please contact your Swing Kid or email kidsfair@umich.edu.
