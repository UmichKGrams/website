Kids-Fair 2010 is HERE!

Today's the day - we'll celebrate our year of reading, writing, swingin', and smilin'! One thousand 1st-5th grade students from ten schools in Ann Arbor, Detroit, and Ypsilanti will be here for the 11th Annual K-grams Kids-Fair.

If you haven't signed up, and you would like to join us, just hop on the Southbound Commuter Bus at any major bus stop and take it all the way to Crisler Arena. We'd love to see you there!

If you have already signed-up, you can check to see which shifts you are registered for by clicking here.

We hope to see you at Crisler!!!
