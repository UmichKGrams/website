Kids-Fair Booth Ideas

Historical Events/Booth Ideas
Dinosaurs

Ice Age
· How are glaciers formed?

Building of the Coliseum
· Explain who the Romans were and why the Coliseum was built. Have the kids pretend they are the emperor and have them design what they would build to impress the world.

Building Pyramids in Egypt
· geometry-based building project

1492: Columbus sails to the Americas
· students could come up with a reason for exploring (lack of resources, etc) then work to draft a petition to a king/queen/leader for funding to explore
· The geography of Columbus' journey possibly charting your own course on a map and seeing where you (the elem. student) would land if you're navigating a ship
· Sacagawea (Native American who helped the pilgrims)

1776: America gains independence, Declaration of Independence
· Have a copy of the declaration of Independence and let kids sign it, make amendments
· make US flags, create your own US flag, read Dec. of Independence

Boston Tea Party

Civil War, Emancipation Proclamation

Gettysburg

Invention of Model T by Henry Ford
· have main parts of a car on paper and have kids put them on a mode of a car (ie: pin the steering wheel on the car)

1817: Founding of U of M!!
· Teach Hail to the victors, Michigan history

1920: Women get the right to vote
· sample voting ballot, vote for the best kids fair booth

1929: Stock market crash, The Great Depression

Invention of small pox vaccine, penicillin

Invention of the airplane
· Show the evolution of the airplane, explain simple aerodynamics, have kids make paper airplanes to show the pointed ones fly better than square ones.

Titanic

Invention of Barbie Doll

Bombing at Pearl Harbor/World War 1
· Battleship type of game, making airplanes

1960s: Dressing up in 60s fashions

1969: 1st Astronaut in space, 1st Man on the moon
· making a space shuttle out of candy or something
· Moonwalk/moon shoes, moon walk made from air mattresses or pillows
· Fake moon rocks
· Make a flag you'll put on the moon
· taste test astronaut food
· Draw pictures of space/moon
· Learn about gravity, solar system
