Thanks for being a K-grams Kids-Fair Buddy! Please read this page carefully,

because it contains everything a Buddy needs to know!

 The Facts:

  • Third Annual K-grams Kids-Fair, Friday, February 9, 2000, at Crisler Arena from approx. 9am – 1pm.
  • Excited elementary kids (K-5) from Ann Arbor, Ypsilanti, and Detroit are coming to interact with you and the plethora of student group sponsored activities (listed on the web).
  • Please see the map on how to get to Crisler Arena. The Southbound Commuter Bus stops all over campus (Pierpont Commons, Michigan Union, CC Little, and more). Make sure to take the Southbound not the Northbound Commuter Bus (they are completely different routes).

  

What to know:

  • If you forget what shifts you’ve signed up for, just check the website: www.umich.edu/~smile Please try to arrive AT LEAST 10 minutes before your shift (earlier the better!).
  • If you are a pen pal, then you will be able to meet your specific little kid pen pal from this year (the little kids will be at the Fair ALL DAY). If not, you will be paired up with an elementary school student.
  • Buddies and little kid pen pals will be split up into rotating groups by color. You will receive your specific color when you arrive to the Kids-Fair. It is important that you keep an upbeat and alert attitude when coming in, because the younger kids might be a little confused when their other pals are coming and going.
  • Bring some cash, so you can buy a spiffy K-grams T-shirt for only $10. Raffle tickets will also be on sale for only $1. You could win a free dinner to Mongolian BBQ, Gratzi’s, Pizza House, and more!
  • Refreshments will be provided only for Buddies who will be at the Kids-Fair all day (9am-2pm).

What to do:

  • Get all of your friends to sign up on-line TODAY to be a Buddy... we still need more big kids!
  • Keep checking the website for FAQ’s and updates on this year’s K-grams Kids-Fair.
  • Either walk down or take a Southbound Commuter Bus (leaves every 10 minutes) to Crisler Arena.
  • Get excited for the biggest and best event of the year! :)
