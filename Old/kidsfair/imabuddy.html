I'm a Kids-Fair Buddy! 
What do I do now???

Buddy Sign-Up General Info FAQs

Thank you for signing up to be a Kids-Fair Buddy! We look forward to seeing you at Crisler Arena on Friday, April 16, 2010. Between now and Kids-Fair, you will be receive email reminders and mailings with important information regarding Kids-Fair. Below is what you'll need to know as a Kids-Fair Buddy. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at kidsfair@umich.edu.

As a Buddy, You Will ...

  • Get excited to attend Kids-Fair on Friday, April 16, 2010 during the time that you signed up for!!
  • Hang out with an elementary school buddy and travel through Crisler Arena with him/her. You will take part in the various student group booths, visit various Karnival booths, and have tons of fun!
  • Meet other college students and become aware of many other student groups on campus

Other Important Info:

  • K-grams Kids-Fair will begin at 9:30am and end at 2:00pm.
  • If you are a pen pal, you will be able to meet your specific little kid pen pal from this year (the little kids will be at the Fair ALL DAY). If you are not a pen pal, you will be paired up with an elementary school student.
  • Buddies and little kid pen pals will be split up into rotating groups by color. You will receive your specific color when you arrive at the Kids-Fair.

What Else Can You Do?

  • Get all of your friends to sign up on-line TODAY to be a Buddy... we still need more big kids!
  • Keep checking the website for FAQ’s and updates on this year’s K-grams Kids-Fair.
  • Walk down or take a Southbound Commuter (http://mbus.pts.umich.edu) The buses leave every 10 minutes to Crisler Arena.
  • Get excited for the biggest and best event of the year...its time to take a trip Through the Ages!
