Who Loves Their Diplomas?
... All the K-grams Kids

After Kids-Fair 2001, all 1,000+ elementary pen pals received a special gift from their pen pals - a diploma.  The diploma recognized that each little kid had completed a great year of K-grams.  Along with the diploma came a picture of the their pen pal, which all the kids enjoyed.  Many were put on their desk, on their wall, and anywhere where they could see them!  Here's what some of the teachers had to say about the diplomas:
.

Many of the kids have taped them to their desks, put them in their portfolios, took them home to share with parents...
We all loved them!!
We made a bulletin board out of them for everyone to enjoy for several months. Then the students took them home. Some of the students have them on the walls of their bedrooms.
