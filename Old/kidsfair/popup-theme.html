Continuing with last year's success, Kids-Fair 2003 will again be incorporating a theme into all of the activities! This year, the theme is:
"A Smile In Time"

As a student group participating in Kids-Fair, you will be planning an "edu-active" (educational & interactive) booth activity that incorporates this historical theme. Once you fill out the online student group application, a Student Group Liaison will contact you and start developing a quality booth activity with you. 

Don't worry about not being able to come up with a booth idea, that's what your Liaison is there for. Just sign up now, and we'll help you do the rest!
