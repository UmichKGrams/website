Be a Kids-Fair Buddy!
Buddy Sign-Up General Info FAQs

Check back in the fall for more information about being a buddy at Kids Fair! Over 1,000 elementary school students are coming to campus to have fun with you and more than 80 student groups. Whether you are currently a K-grams pen pal or not, YOU can be a Kids-Fair Buddy! We will pair you up with a 1st-5th grade student to navigate through educational and interactive activities at Crisler Arena. Of course, if you are already a pen pal, we will match you up with your pal from this year!  The commitment is simple, yet extremely fulfilling for both you and your elementary buddy.

The Kids Fair theme for 2011 will be announced in the fall! Mingle throughout Crisler Arena and have fun at all the various booths!

We know that not every pen pal can make it to the Kids-Fair for the entire time. For pen pals that cannot make it the entire time, we ask you to find a friend to come to Kids-Fair for the shifts that you cannot attend. 

This is also a great opportunity to get a student group involved by providing Kids-Fair Buddies. Whether or not your group is sponsoring a booth, members from your group can sign up to be Buddies for the day. Your student group will also be recognized as participating in this event. Being a Buddy is a rewarding and educational experience for both you and our elementary school friends!
