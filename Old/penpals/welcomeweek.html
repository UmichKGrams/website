Welcome Week

There's no way you can miss K-grams during Welcome Week - we're everywhere! This is the perfect chance to sign up to be a pen pal or volunteer, so make sure you visit us at one of these fun events!
.
September 2nd - September 6th is the first week that you can sign up to be involved with K-grams for the 2004-2005 year! In addition to signing up to be a pen pal in your residence hall, you will find K-grams at the various Welcome Week activities. You can sign up to get involved with K-grams right away by visiting us at the following events:
.
K-grams Recess and Escapade
Thursday, September 2nd:  Come visit us on the Diag and play extreme dodgeball from 11am-5pm. Here you can learn more about K-grams and be the FIRST person to sign up to be a pen pal! That night, head over to the Amer's courtyard at the Union. While you sign up to be a pen pal, you can also join us for a dance party, eat some food, and meet some new friends!
.
Maize Craze
Friday, September 3rd: Join us as we gear up for the first home football game of the season at Maize Craze. This campus-wide pep rally is a perfect to enjoy free food, meet new people, and show us your U of M spirit!
 
Union Carnival and Pre-Class Bash
Monday, September 6th: Join us as the Union celebrates it's 100th year. We will be working at the hot dog booth and will also be representing K-grams in the dunk tank! Be sure to stop by and get free food and dunk us! Afterwards, you can head over for some more free food at Palmer Field for the Pre-Class Bash. Look for K-grammers walking around in their maize shirts to find out how you can get invovled in K-grams.
 
Festifall!
Thursday, September 9th: This festival of student organizations is where you can learn about and sign-up to get involved in the 800+ student groups on campus. K-grams will be alive and smilin' at Festifall! You can sign up to be a pen pal, find out more information about BookMARK (Mentoring And Reading with Kids), classroom projects, and Kids-Fair. Get ready to swing into the new year with K-grams :) 
.
Other Events
K-grams will be participating in various other events that are taking place during your first week on campus. Be sure to find us at Escapade, the ice cream socials in your residence halls, Pre-Class Bash, and many of the other Welcome Week events. You can always stop by our office (4217 Michigan Union) to see us too!
