K-grams Recess!!

Do you miss four-square? Do you wish that you could have recess during your Chem 101 Lecture? Here's your chance to take a break and act like a kid again!

K-grams is teaming up with Comcast and the Game Show Network to bring Extreme Dodgeball to campus. On Thursday, September 2nd from 11am-5pm bring your friends, your roommates, or the random people you meet on the street to the Diag and see if you have what it takes!

Apply to be a K-grams Pen Pal!

If you live in a traditional residence halls you can apply to be a K-grams pen pal! As a pen pal, you have a chance to act as a mentor, meet an awesome little kid, and make friends in your residence hall. Find out about more imformation about being a pen pal.

Other ways to get involved...

There are many other ways to become involved in K-grams! Starting in late September, K-grams BookMARK offers opportunities for U-M students to go into elementary classrooms and mentor kids. Sign-ups begin in late September, but you can learn more about becoming a BookMARK volunteer.

If you are interested in becoming more involved with K-grams, email smile@umich.edu for further information. We can't wait to see you soon.

Please be sure to continue checking our Calendar of Events for K-grams happenings.
