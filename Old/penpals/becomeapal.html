Become A Pen Pal!

Becoming a pen pal is very easy! Just follow the instructions below and you'll be Reading, Writing, Swingin' & Smilin' in no time!

To become a K-grams pen pal for 2009-2010:

  • First, you must be a resident in a traditional on-campus residence hall. (However, if you aren't, keep on reading, there are still many ways in which you can be involved!)
  • Second, if you would like to be a pen pal starting in Fall 2009, you can begin signing up during Welcome Week. You can visit us at one of the many Welcome Week events or pick up an application in your residence hall. You cannot sign-up online to be a pen pal (but you can sign-up online for just about everything else!)
  • Third, fill out the application (don't worry - no hard questions!) and drop it off at your hall's front desk!
    Priority is given to returning pen pals and those willing to commit to other projects (BookMARK, committees, Kids-Fair, hall events, etc).
  • Fourth, you will be contacted by email that you are a part of K-grams! Just come to the Pen Pal Kickoff in your residence hall to find out everything about the program and who you'll be writing to!


To get more involved:

Living off-campus or not being a pen pal does not need to limit your involvement with K-grams.  As a matter of fact, there are many other ways to be involved with our group other than being a pen pal.  You can also get your affiliated student group or hall involved, or even help create your own program with us!  Check it out...

  • Any individual or group can volunteer in BookMARK, our weekly mentoring and reading program, or in any of our other volunteer opportunities.
  • As a pen pal you can also volunteer to help your Swing Kid or other organizers in your own Hall.  The most needed (and rewarding way) to get more involved is to help plan projects with your pen pals!
  • Also, the Kids Programming Council is always looking for volunteers who want to make a strong commitment to actually plan and organize  the various K-grams programs. :)  
  • Finally, if you are unsure of what you want to be involved in, contact us and we will get back to you!
