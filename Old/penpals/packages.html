Package Examples!!
Use these to get ideas for your packages

 

Fun in the Sun!

What you Need: 2 large pieces of yellow posterboard
Markers
Stapler
Glue
String/Ribbon

How to: Cut two large circles of yellow posterboard.  Use the remaining posterboard to form the sun's rays.  Make enough rays so each child's name can be written on one side of a ray.  Staple the "inside" onto the circles.  Decorate one circle with the names of the big kid pen pals surrounding the K-grams logo. Make the other circle into a big smiley face (wearing sunglasses).  Staple the two circles together, leaving enough room to insert the letters and punch two holes so the string or ribbon can be inserted as a hanger.

X-tra Ideas: You may need to add something in the middle of the sun to better hold the letters.  The letters are inserted at the top where the string to hang the sun is attached.


Flower Pot!

What you Need: 1 flower pot
Two cardboard circles per flower (6 inch diameter)
One wooden dowel per two flowers (stem)
One Styrofoam disc
Green and yellow paper
Fake Easter-basket grass
Tape
long white envelopes

How to: Put Styrofoam disk into pot so it covers the opening.  Break the dowel into two pieces and cover it with green paper.  Cover the cardboard circles with yellow paper.  Put the letters in envelopes and write each big kid's name at one end.  Poke one circle onto end of dowel and tape into place.  Tape the envelopes on top of the circle so the end with name sticks out.  Tape the other circle on top.  Poke the dowel into the pot.  Cover the top of the Styrofoam with fake grass.

X-tra Ideas: Give class soil and flower seeds so they can grow plants.
Paint/Decorate the pot



Spooky Halloween!

What you Need: Paper Bag with handles
Black, orange, white, and yellow construction paper
Glitter
Glue, tape & scissors
Markers

How To: Cover the bag with black construction paper.
Make a spooky Halloween scene on one side of the bag with
construction paper and put the class name on the other side.

X-tra Ideas: Halloween pencils are inexpensive and
fun for the kids!
