Applications

There are many ways to get involved with K-grams through volunteering and/or taking on different roles in the organization. We encourage you to take a look at what there is to offer, and apply for a position on the KPC! Listed below are the steps that you will need to take to complete the application process:

  1. Review the Personnel Chart and role descriptions to see what positions are open and what each one entails.
     
  2. Download the appropriate application (see below) in Microsoft Word format. After downloading the file, type directly into the document (this method is preferred), or print it out and write on it. If you have trouble accessing the file, please pick up a hard copy at our office.
      
  3. When completed: turn in your application at the K-grams office (4217 Michigan Union - 4th floor). KPC applications are due Thursday, March 12th, 2009. Swing Kid applications will be available in April; there will also be a re-application process in the fall.
     
  4. You may be required to have an interview (or two) with our selection committee; you will indicate which days work best for you on your application and will be emailed your interview time.
