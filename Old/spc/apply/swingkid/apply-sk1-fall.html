Swing Kid Application!

Thank you for your interest in being a K-grams Swing Kid!  This is a very fun role, but you will also have some important responsibilities.  Make sure to read all the information before filling out the application.

K-grams is accepting applications for Swing Kids for the 2003-2004 year! There are many ways to get involved with K-grams through volunteering and/or taking on different roles in the organization. We strongly encourage you to review the swing kid role description before you apply. You may then fill out our online application.  If you have any further questions, please contact at smile@umich.edu.

As a Swing Kid, you supervise the letter collection each month and encourage pen pal involvement. You are assigned to group of 20-30 college pen pals who correspond with one elementary school classroom. You ensure that all college pen pals turn in their letters on the appropriate Monday due dates throughout the year. In addition, you design and create packages in which the monthly letters are placed and delivered to the elementary school classroom. You will meet bi-weekly with the Hall Heads and other Swing Kids to coordinate and execute classroom projects. Developing a strong relationship with your pen pals, you and your residence hall team will establish an enjoyable community of learning in the residence halls.
