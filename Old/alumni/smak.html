Smile Michigan Alumni of K-grams
SMAK Overview

SMAK, as we so lovingly call our alumni association, is a vehicle for you to
stay connected to the happenings of Michigan K-grams, as well as the people
with whom you served throughout your year.


Welcome to the wonderful world of alumni through K-grams at the University of Michigan. To be a member of SMAK (Smile Michigan Alumni of K-grams), you simply need to have been a part of K-grams at Michigan in some capacity - whether as a pen pal, director, volunteer, Kids-Fair buddy or parent! You also must be a non-student at the University of Michigan (either graduated or otherwise removed).

This is what you should expect from being a member:

  • Quarterly email newsletter about the happenings of U-M K-grams, special programs, quotes from teachers and SPC members, and highlights of other alumni activities
  • A network of your peers from college and K-grams
  • In the future, we should have career networking opportunities, ways to get more involved as alumni, starting your own K-grams and much more!

Please get your alumni friends to sign-up ASAP and join our membership list!

To get involved simply submit the virtual form!

If you have any questions or comments, please contact:
our SMAK Chair at smak@umich.edu
