Mentoring And Reading with Kids!

 (Check out our participating classrooms)

BookMARK (Mentoring And Reading with Kids) is an incredibly enjoyable, simple, and rewarding program that you can join through K-grams! Volunteer to reach out to local elementary school students by traveling to the school and reading with them or helping them with other special projects. This is extremely flexible and lets you go to the school as much or as often as you want. You will find that everyone benefits from this program while giving you an opportunity to have fun with kids too!.

Important Notes (please read before submitting your signup!):
  • All BookMARK Sessions meet at THE CUBE (between the Union and the LSA Building), at the listed starting time.
  • All transportation is provided. Transportation time is included within the listed times.
  • If for some reason, you CANNOT attend a session that you are signed up for, please notify your coordinator as soon as possible, or email bookmark@umich.edu.
  • You will receive a reminder email from your session coordinator prior to your session.
  • * denotes that a session is full and has been closed. X denotes that there is no session that week.
  • If you have any additional questions, please email bookmark@umich.edu
