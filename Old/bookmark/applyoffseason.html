BookMARK!
Mentoring And Reading with Kids!

BookMARK (Mentoring And Reading with Kids) is an incredibly enjoyable, simple, and rewarding program that you can join through K-grams! Volunteer to reach out to local elementary school students by traveling to the school and reading with them or helping them with other special projects. This is extremely flexible and lets you go to the school as much or as often as you want. You will find that everyone benefits from this program while giving you an opportunity to have fun with kids too!.

K-grams BookMARK signups will be available by early October and sessions will start mid-October. As always, please email bookmark@umich.edu with any questions. Thanks!
