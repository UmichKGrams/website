Welcome to Bryant's K-grams page! These 1st - 2nd grade kids know how to party!  In fact, every year the students of Mrs. Kitzmann's class hold a special party for all of their pen pals!  The party is complete with lots of food and lots of fun activities.  But that's not all that's at Bryant, we've got 3 other classes that are just as fun, so if you have the chance, make sure you get to know all of these cool kids!
