Welcome to Northside's K-grams page!  Northside, located in Ann Arbor, houses 5 classes of K-grams pen pals.  There are lots of great projects going on there this year.   The favorite so far would have to be the Fall Festival with cookie decorating, games, treasure hunts, and all kinds of fun!  Also, look at other programs Northside is involved with, such as their BookMARK classroom.
