Welcome to Holmes' K-grams page! Holmes Elementary is one of our two schools in the Willow Run School District and joined K-grams way back in the Winter of 1998.  Holmes is special as they are paired up with both Couzens pals and the pan pals from Circle K.  There are always fun projects at Holmes, like Pumpkin carving and valentine making, so make sure you check them out!
