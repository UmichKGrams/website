K-grams Presents:
Extreme Dodgeball!


Read the Michigan Daily Article:
Students dodge balls to become
mentors for elementary schools

On Thursday, Sep. 2, 2004 on the diag, K-grams, along with the Game Show Network and Comcast, presented Extreme Dodgeball as part of the K-grams Welcome Week Recess event and party.

Games were played all afternoon long with 4-7 people on a team competing for prizes and the right to brag as a dodgeball champion. Among the prizes folks collected at the K-grams table were Sony MP3 players, digital cameras, T-shirts, X-Box games, candy, food, and more!
