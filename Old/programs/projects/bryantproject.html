Project: Halloween Fun!  Date: 10/22/01
Project Head: Kayta Melkote

The kids were broken up into groups of 5 or 6 and each was given a pumpkin.  They were asked to estimate the weight, circumference, and the number of seeds for each pumpkin.  Next they carved the pumpkin with our help and found out the actual weight circumference, and number of seeds.

Project: Make Your Own Storybook  Date: 12/19/01
Project Head: Kayta Melkote

The 1st graders in Mrs. Brimacombe/Harrington's class created a huge alphabet storybook, as they learn the different letters and words that correspond with each letter. Each student contributed to the storybook by designing a page for a certain letter of the alphabet. The teacher assigned each student a different letter, and the kids had to draw pictures and write a story using words that begin with their special letter.

Project: Create a Country  Date: 12/19/01
Project Head: Kayta Melkote

Mrs. Kitzmann's 2nd graders have incorporated the Kids-Fair 2002 international theme into their curriculum by creatin their own special country called "Nevis." Through this project, the college volunteers worked with the little kids to establish the government, dress, food, culture, currency, laws, language, and sports for this country. The kids used their imagination to develop a country that they will live in until Kids-Fair.
