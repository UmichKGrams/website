Project: Multiple Sunny Days  Date: 10/26/01
Project Head: Payal Patel

We helped 5th graders learn about multiple intelligences of the brain, which is one of the units they are studying in science.  We had 7 groups for the 7 intelligences and 7 different stations with supplies pertaining to their intelligence.  At the stations the kids interpreted a sunny day and then shared their ideas with the class.  We also constructed a large model of the brain to show its different features.

Project: Popcorn Food Chain & Fruit Stamps  Date: 11/30/01
Project Head: Payal Patel

We demonstrated the Food Chain to the kids through a simulation with popcorn.  Each child was given a role to play and we saw how popcorn trickled through the chain just like the real food chain.  After that we continued with an activity where we made fruit and vegetable stamps and tried to distinguish the two as seed bearing and non-seed bearing.
