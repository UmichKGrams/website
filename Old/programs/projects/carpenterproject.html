Project: Winter Wonderland!  Date: 12/7/01
Project Head: Sara Greenwood

Mrs. Luce/Freeman's multi-age classroom (3rd-5th grade) wrote haiku poems about their favorite things to do during the winter. They also cutout snowflakes and decorated them with markers, glitter, ribbons, and other art supplies.
