K-grams Family Night @ Women's Gymnastics!

Friday, January 21st, 2005 at 7:00pm will be the Third Annual K-grams Family Night at the Michigan Women's Gymnastics Meet at Crisler Arena! As the Wolverines battle the Penn State Nittany Lions, K-grams will bring the fun! All K-grams kids, families, and friends are cordially invited to attend this special event. This will be a unique opportunity to see two rival teams compete, hang out in Crisler Arena, and have fun with all of your K-grams friends.

For more info on this year's EVENT, check this out!


The gymnasts will visit the elementary schools and gear up the kids for the meet! They can't wait to see all the K-grams kids in the stands cheering them on. All college students and K-grams elementary students are invited to attend this event. College students get in free with their student ID, and the elem kids will also get in free with a complimentary blue ticket which they'll receive very soon!

Get your family and friends together for this night, no registration is required and all K-grams kids get in FREE! This will also be a great chance to preview Crisler Arena for Kids-Fair, which will take place on March 11th.

 
Five Great Reasons to Come to K-grams Family Night
We'll pick an "Honorary Gymnast" to sit with the team during the meet
Raffle for prizes, including an exclusive visit to a Women's Gymnastics Practice and a pizza party at your school
Autographs and pictures with the team after the meet
Two-story inflatable "M"
T -shirt giveaways, pom-poms, face tattoos, calendars, trading cards, and more!



For more information on this special event, please visit: www.kgrams.org/gym or email knastics@umich.edu.
