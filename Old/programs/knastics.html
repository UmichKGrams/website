K-grams Night @ Women's Gymnastics!

Friday, January 27, 2006
 

Friday, January 27, 2006 is the Fourth Annual K-grams Night ("K-nastics") at the Michigan Women's Gymnastics Meet in Crisler Arena!  The Wolverines will battle Big Ten rival Ohio State, and K-grams will be sponsoring games, prizes, and other fun throughout the night. All students and staff are invited to this special event, and get in free with an MCard!

Click here for complete information on K-grams Night!

Attend K-grams Night, Help Raise Money for Kids Programs!

For each person that attends K-grams Night, Comcast will donate $1 to help fund Kids Programs in our communities!


We answer the big "W"s for you below:
  • What - K-grams Night at the University of Michigan Women’s Gymnastics Meet. Watch the team compete and have some fun in between with your friends. The gymnasts compete on the floor, balance beam, uneven bars, and vault, while the crowd enjoys their performances and extra games and give-a-ways in the stands!

  • When - Friday, January 27, 2006 from 7:15pm – 9:30pm (get there early!)

  • Where - Crisler Arena (students section is Section 39)

  • Who - Everyone is welcome to attend this event. Along with many U-M students, K-grams elementary pen pals have also been invited to this event. It will be a great time for kids of all ages!

    The gymnastics team is led by senior All Americans Becca Clauson, Jenny Deiley, and Lauren Mirkovich.

  • Why - Watch some amazing Olympic-level gymnastics, have fun with college friends, and raise money for K-grams! (Comcast will donate $1 per person who attends to fund Kids Programs.)

  • Tickets - All U-M students get in free with MCard.
