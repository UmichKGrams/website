October 20th is Kids-Day!

 
Mark your calendars kids, because on Friday, October 20, a fun new K-grams event will be taking place. It's called K-Day, short for Kids Day. Fifty elementary school kids from Ford Elementary in Ypsilanti will be coming to explore U of M's beautiful campus. The fifth graders will visit the Big House, tour the Art Museum, and get to enjoy the experience of eating in Markley's luxurious cafeteria. On top of all this fun, they will get to visit MoJo, the rez hall Ford Elementary is paired up with. K-Day gives Ford Elementary School students a wonderful opportunity to experience a few aspects of college life.

If you are interested in joining the Markley lunch or helping with the rez hall tour contact smileschools@umich.edu
  The Schedule for the day is as follows:
w 8:45 am Leave Ford Elementary School
w    9:15 - 9:30 Arrive at Michigan Stadium
w    10:00 - 10:45 Guided tour of the Art Museum
w    11:00 - 11:30 Walk over to Markley and learn fun U of M facts
w    11:30 - 12:15  Lunch in the Markley North Dining Room
w 12:15 - 12:30 Finish up Lunch, gather kids to walk to MoJo
w 12:30 - 1:00 Sweet tour of MoJo
w    1:10  Buses pick-up kids behind CCRB and smile!
