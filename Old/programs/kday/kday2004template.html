K-grams Kids-Day
The Kids are Coming on November 4th!

On Friday November 4th, Mrs. Warner's 5th graders from Ford Elementary school will be coming to the U of M campus for a whole day of fun! During the day the kids will do everything from getting to stand in Michigan Stadium to eating lunch in a residence hall cafeteria (which means all the cookies they can eat!).  They'll tour the campus, see a real college rez hall, and meet lots of college students!

We are looking for excited pen pals and volunteers to help out with K-Day!  If you are interested in being a buddy for the day, please sign up to help! Volunteers can sign up in shifts, or help out for the entire day!  This year marks the seventh K-Day, so make sure you check it out!
