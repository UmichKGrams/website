Join us for K-grams Night @ UofM Women's Gymnastics Meet!

Friday, March 14th, 2003 at 7:30pm will be K-grams Night at the Michigan Women's Gymnastics Meet at Crisler Arena! As the Wolverines battle the Nebraska Cornhuskers, K-grams will be bringing the fun! All University of Michigan students, as well as all K-grams elementary kids, families, and friends are cordially invited to attend this special event. This will be a unique opportunity to see two top-5 nationally ranked teams compete, hang out at the University of Michigan, and have fun with all of your K-grams friends.

 

Get your all of your friends together for this night! Not only will this be an amazing gymnastics meet, but it will also be a great chance to preview Crisler Arena for Kids-Fair, which will take place one week later. Keep your eyes open during the winter for information on the meet, K-grams projects with UofM gymnasts, and more fun!
