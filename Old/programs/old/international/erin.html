Why did you choose to spend a year abroad, and what made you choose Germany?

I chose to spend a year abroad because I really wanted to experience living and studying in another country. I chose Germany because I will have the opportunity to improve my German speaking skills. I want to be surrounded by the German language and culture all the time!

Have you ever been outside of the United States? If so, where else have you been?

I've been to London, England once and Germany twice. I went to Germany with my family for a two-week vacation. The second time I went with my high school as an exchange student and had the chance to live with a German family for one month. This trip to Germany, however, will be the longest time that I've been away from home!

What are a couple of things that you hope to accomplish in Germany?

I hope to make lots of memories and meet new people. I also want to be able to speak German fast and really well by the end of the year. I would like to visit many places in Europe besides Germany-- France, Italy, and more!

What are you most nervous about?

I think I'm nervous because all my classes I take will be in another language! Hopefully I'll be able to understand the important stuff.

Name one thing that you will miss about Ann Arbor or the US.

I'll definitely miss friends and family, but I'll also miss driving a car and going to Saturday football games in the Michigan Stadium.

What was your favorite activity to do during recess?

I really liked to play on the tire swing.

Please list some hobbies and/or activities that you enjoy.

In my free time I like to write letters to my pen pals all over the world. I enjoy spending time with my family, hanging out with my friends, and taking walks with my mom.

Anything you would like to say to your classroom?

I'm so excited that I can share my year with you! I hope by the end you will know a lot about me and Germany!
