K-grams Night @ Women's Gymnastics!

On Friday, March 14th, 2003 over 2,500 gymnastics and K-grams enthusiasts came out to Crisler Arena on the U-M campus for K-grams Night at the University of Michigan Women's Gymnastics Meet. The Wolverines competed against the Nebraska Cornhuskers in four events - vault, uneven bars, balance beam, and floor.

For more info on this year's EVENT, check this out!


Besides seeing these amazing girls flip, dance, tumble and more, the fans were also treated to lots of fun from K-grams. K-grams volunteers tossed t-shirts into the crowd and raffled off a number of cool prizes. Congratulations to our raffle winners!

Honorary Gymnast: Levi M., Carpenter Elementary

Gymnastics T-shirt Autographed by the team: Maya F., Northside Elementary & Steven C., Bryant Elementary

Visit to a U-M Gymnastics Practice: Keely F., Dicken Elementary & Brittany C., Holmes Elementary

Pizza Party With the Gymnastics Team: Justice K., Dicken Elementary
Before the meet, the Gym team was also able to visit the K-grams schools to meet the kids and hand out free tickets to all of our K-grams kids. The classes, in turn, made cool banners for the gymnasts, which were hung up at the meet. Special thanks to everyone who came out to K-grams Night, our flower kids, everyone who participated in the dizzy relay race, and especially the gym team, Head Coach Bev Plocki, and Mark Riordan!
