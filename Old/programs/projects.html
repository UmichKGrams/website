Classroom Projects

Two line description
Currently, we are organizing projects with each elementary school classroom... These projects range from science experiments, arts and crafts projects, learning about different countries, and the list goes on! They provide essential time for the pen pals (and other college students) to interact and get to know each other better. This page should give you an idea of what some of our recent projects were like! If you are interested in getting involved in future projects, check out the Calendar of Events, or email smile@umich.edu.
