Hall Heads

Hall Heads are responsible for the maintenance of the pen pal program in one to two assigned residence halls and elementary schools. This includes the responsibility of collecting all letters from swing kids, checking the letters for appropriate content, delivering the letters to the elementary teachers, picking up the letters from elementary students, delivering them to the college aged pen pals. Additionally, Hall Heads are responsible for attending a weekly meeting as well as all other expectations of the KPC as a whole.
